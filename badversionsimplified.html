Concordian Translator

About Concordian

Concordian is an artificial constructed language with simple rules. This tool translates words between English and Concordian forms according to the mapping below.

  • Phonetic spelling: Letters are replaced by consistent equivalents.
  • Special vocabulary: Some common words are mapped directly for flavor.

Word List & Letter Mapping

a→ka b→tu c→si d→da e→re f→fi g→gi h→ha i→ni j→jo k→ku l→li m→ma n→ne o→lo p→pa q→qu r→ri s→sa t→ta u→mu v→va w→wa x→xa y→ya z→za

Special vocabulary: hello→salena, world→verda, friend→samanu, yes→aya, no→inau, thank you→areku, peace→konrda
